TECHVAULT INC. EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK – 2024 EDITION
================================================

1. INTRODUCTION
---------------
Welcome to TechVault Inc.! This handbook outlines the policies, benefits, and
expectations that govern your employment. Please read it carefully. If you have
questions, contact the People Operations team at hr@techvaultinc.com.

2. REMOTE WORK POLICY
----------------------
TechVault supports flexible working arrangements. Full-time employees may work
remotely for up to 3 days per week. Remote work beyond this limit requires
written manager approval submitted at least five business days in advance through
the HR portal. Employees must maintain a dedicated, distraction-free workspace
and be reachable during core hours (10:00 AM – 3:00 PM in their local time zone).
Temporary fully-remote arrangements (e.g., illness, travel) can be authorised by
a manager for up to two consecutive weeks.

3. ANNUAL LEAVE
---------------
All full-time employees receive 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Leave accrues at approximately 2.08 days per month, starting from the first day
of employment. No more than 10 unused days may be carried over into the following
year. Requests must be submitted via the HR portal with at least 10 business days'
notice for leave longer than three consecutive days.

Statutory public holidays are in addition to the 25-day entitlement. Part-time
employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis.

4. HEALTH AND WELLBEING BENEFITS
----------------------------------
TechVault provides comprehensive health benefits:

  • Medical Insurance: Full medical coverage, including dental and vision,
    becomes active after the 90-day probationary period. Employees may add
    dependants during the enrolment window within 30 days of the coverage
    activation date.

  • Mental Health Support: All employees have access to six free counselling
    sessions per year through the Employee Assistance Programme (EAP).

  • Gym Subsidy: TechVault reimburses up to £50/month toward gym membership
    or fitness classes, claimable via Concur expensing.

5. EQUIPMENT POLICY
--------------------
On your first day, TechVault will provide a company-issued laptop (macOS or
Windows, as agreed with your manager) pre-configured by the IT team. The laptop
remains company property at all times. If you leave TechVault—whether voluntarily
or otherwise—you must return the laptop along with any peripherals (monitor, dock,
keyboard) within 5 business days of your last working day. Failure to do so may
result in the cost being deducted from your final salary in accordance with
applicable employment law. Personal use of the laptop is permitted provided it
does not breach the Acceptable Use Policy.

6. CODE OF CONDUCT
-------------------
TechVault is committed to a respectful, inclusive workplace. All employees are
expected to:

  • Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with dignity and respect.
  • Report harassment, discrimination, or bullying promptly to their manager
    or the People Operations team. Anonymous reports can be submitted via
    the Ethics Hotline at ethics@techvaultinc.com.
  • Never share confidential company or client information outside authorised
    channels.
  • Comply with the Data Protection Policy and GDPR obligations at all times.

Violations of the Code of Conduct may lead to disciplinary action up to and
including dismissal.

7. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
-----------------------
Formal performance reviews are conducted twice a year: mid-year (June) and
year-end (December). Both the employee and their manager complete a self-
assessment and a manager assessment in the HR system. Outcomes inform salary
adjustments, promotions, and personal development plans.

8. PROBATIONARY PERIOD
-----------------------
All new employees undergo a 90-day probationary period. During this time,
either party may terminate employment with one week's notice. At the end of
the probation, the manager will confirm the outcome in writing. Successful
completion unlocks full health benefits, annual leave accrual credit from
day one, and access to the pension scheme.

9. AMENDMENTS
--------------
TechVault reserves the right to update this handbook at any time. Employees
will be notified of material changes via email and the company intranet. It
is each employee's responsibility to stay informed of current policies.
